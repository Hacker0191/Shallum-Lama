ABOUT
My name is Shallum Lama and I am a songwriter, guitarist and producer originally from Pedong Kalimpong India .
Shallum first began publishing his songs written in English, and then continued on to release his songs written in Nepali & Hindi. His genre,Rnb, Pop, Pop-Rock… Shallum first released his work publicly in 2020, with the release of his Nepali single ‘Hashi Dew Na’, Followed with the release of ‘Illusions’, which was later followed by other major releases ‘Daar, Ugyalo’, ‘Sahunay Jhari’, among which ‘Timro Pratiksa’ viral song was well loved throughout the Country and globally.
